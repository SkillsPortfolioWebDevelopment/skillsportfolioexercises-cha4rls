Plot Casts Interesting Facts Reviews

Plot

Lucifer is an American urban fantasy television series based on the DC Comics character Lucifer Morningstar.

The series revolves around the life of Lucifer, who left Hell to stay in Los Angeles where he owns a club named Lux. He then met LAPD Detective Chloe Decker after an accident happened just outside his club. After helping LAPD solve their case, Lucifer accepts the invitation to work with Detective Chloe as a consultant to the department.
